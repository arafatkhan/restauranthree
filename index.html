Our Story

Founded in 1985, Pizza House has been serving authentic Italian cuisine to our community for over 40 years. What started as a small family-owned pizzeria has grown into a beloved local institution.

Our secret is simple: we use only the freshest ingredients, traditional recipes passed down through generations, and a genuine passion for Italian cuisine.

Every pizza is hand-crafted with love and baked in our traditional wood-fired oven, giving it that distinctive smoky flavor and perfect crust that our customers have come to love.
